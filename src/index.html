GET YOUTUBE SUBSCRIBERS

THESE ARE THE FOLLOWING APIs PROVIDED BY THE APP.


1. GET/subscribers: Response with an array of subscribers. (in the form of Object)

2. GET/subscribers/names: Response with an array of subscribers with the field 'name' and 'subscribed channel'. (in the form of Object)

3. GET/subscribers/:id: Response with a subscriber of given id. (in the form of Object)
